Touch of the Past

The Touch of the Past wall is an interactive walkthrough of how Ancient Egyptians made bread in the royal palace. This was an incentive proposed by the Nicholson museum. Make learning more entertaining. Internal analysis had found many patrons barely absorbed any information housed within the museum. The solution was to provide the users with a unique experience of a conductive wallpaper which explained the process with animations upon the users interaction.
